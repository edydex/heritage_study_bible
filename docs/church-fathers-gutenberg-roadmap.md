# Church Fathers Gutenberg Roadmap

This list is based on the publisher catalog block that appeared after `INDEXES.` in `public/data/books/city-of-god.txt`.

## Classification notes

- `Core Church Fathers / Patristic authors`: broadly recognized as Fathers or major patristic writers.
- `Patristic-era but not a single Father work`: compilations, apocrypha, liturgies, or pseudepigraphal collections.
